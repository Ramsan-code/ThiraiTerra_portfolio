# Project Proposal: ThiraiTerra
**The Professional Infrastructure for the New Era of Cinema**

---

## Executive Summary
ThiraiTerra is a strategic marketplace platform designed to digitize and professionalize the Sri Lankan film industry. By providing a centralized digital ecosystem for talent discovery, project investment, and professional networking, ThiraiTerra eliminates the inefficiencies of traditional word-of-mouth workflows. Our MVP focuses on a high-prestige digital directory and secure communication layer, laying the foundation for a full-cycle production hiring engine.

---

## 1. Problem Statement
The Sri Lankan film ecosystem is currently hindered by:
- **Fragmentation**: Talent discovery relies on decentralized social media groups and personal contact lists.
- **Opacity**: Lack of verified portfolios and skill sets makes hiring a high-risk activity for producers.
- **Invisibility**: Skilled local technicians struggle to showcase their work to international co-production partners.
- **Inefficiency**: Pre-production sourcing takes weeks, increasing overhead costs for independent and studio films.

---

## 2. The Solution
ThiraiTerra provides a specialized professional marketplace that offers:
- **Verified Digital Portfolios**: Cinematic-grade profiles for directors, technicians, and artists.
- **Advanced Talent Directory**: Multidimensional filtering (role, gear, availability, location).
- **Project Matchmaking**: A secure portal for producers and investors to vet scripts and creative teams.
- **Direct Communication**: Integrated email and messaging infrastructure for the MVP.

---

## 3. Market Opportunity
- **Addressable Market**: $50M+ annual spend in Sri Lankan film, commercial, and digital media production.
- **Target Audience**: 10,000+ film professionals and 200+ active production houses in Sri Lanka.
- **Growth Potential**: 25% annual increase in digital adoption within the creative sector.

---

## 4. Revenue Model & Unit Economics
ThiraiTerra utilizes a tiered subscription model (SaaS) supplemented by transactional fees in later phases.

### Subscription Tiers (Monthly)
| Tier | Pricing (LKR) | Pricing (USD) | Key Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Indie Talent** | Free | Free | Basic Profile, 1 Portfolio Reel, Job Discovery |
| **Pro Creator** | 2,500 | $10 | Verified Badge, Featured Placement, Analytics |
| **Studio/Agency**| 15,000 | $60 | Unlimited Postings, Advanced Filters, Team Tools |

### Future Monetization
- **Transaction Fees**: 5-10% commission on project-based hires processed via the platform.
- **Featured Listings**: Pay-per-day placement for urgent casting calls or equipment rentals.

---

## 5. Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

### Phase 1: Establish Liquidity (Months 1-3)
- **Anchor Talent Program**: Partner with 50 high-profile Sri Lankan directors and DOPs to seed the platform.
- **Niche Focus**: Target the Colombo-based commercial and indie film sectors first.
- **Referral Loop**: "Verified Invite" system where current members vouch for new talent.

### Phase 2: Community Integration (Months 4-6)
- **Institutional Partnerships**: Collaborate with the Sri Lanka Film Corporation and film schools (e.g., Sripalee).
- **Workshops & Events**: Host "Digital Portfolio" workshops to onboard traditional technicians.

### Phase 3: Regional Expansion (Year 2)
- **South Asian Bridge**: Expand to the Tamil and Malayalam film markets (South India) to facilitate cross-border hiring.
- **Investor Portal**: Launch the "Pitch-Deck Marketplace" for project financing.

---

## 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Liquidity**: Ratio of job applications to job postings (Target: 15:1).
- **Verification Rate**: Percentage of profiles with verified portfolios (Target: 60%).
- **Retention**: Monthly Active Talent (MAT) returning to update reels or availability.
- **Hiring Success**: Number of successful contracts facilitated through the platform.

---

## 7. Technical Implementation Details
### Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js 15, React, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion (for premium UI/UX).
- **Backend**: REST API with Node.js/Express.
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (Prisma ORM) for structured talent data.
- **Storage**: AWS S3/Cloudfront for high-bitrate portfolio reels.
- **Security**: RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) for Investors vs. Talent.

### MVP Features
- Responsive profile builder with video embedding.
- Global search with role-based filtering.
- Transactional email notifications via Resend/SendGrid.

## 9. Risk Analysis & Mitigation
- **Adoption Risk**: Traditional industry players may resist digital change. *Mitigation: High-touch onboarding and free "Indie" tier.*
- **Data Privacy**: Handling sensitive portfolio data and contact info. *Mitigation: End-to-end encryption and GDPR-aligned data policies.*
- **Quality Control**: Risk of low-quality profiles. *Mitigation: Tiered verification system and community reporting.*

## 11. Competitive Differentiation
- **Hyper-Local Focus**: Unlike generic global platforms (e.g., LinkedIn or Backstage), ThiraiTerra is architected specifically for the Sri Lankan ecosystem, integrating local rate cards and technical union standards.
- **Verification Engine**: A multi-step vetting process by industry veterans ensures that portfolio data is authentic, significantly reducing hiring risks for international co-productions.
- **High-Prestige UI**: A cinematic design aesthetic that mirrors the quality of the industry it serves, attracting top-tier talent and high-net-worth investors.

---

## 12. Operational Workflows
### For Talent
1. **Onboarding**: Secure profile creation with cinematic reel embedding.
2. **Discovery**: AI-assisted project recommendations based on skill set and gear.
3. **Engagement**: Direct application and secure messaging with production houses.

### For Producers
1. **Sourcing**: Multidimensional filtering (role, equipment, location, availability).
2. **Management**: Centralized dashboard for applicant tracking and portfolio review.
3. **Hiring**: Standardized digital contracts and integrated payment milestones.

### For Investors
1. **Vetting**: Access to a curated gallery of project pitches and team credentials.
2. **Transparency**: Real-time production updates and milestone tracking.
3. **Financing**: Secure portals for investment transactions and equity management.
